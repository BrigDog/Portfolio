2D platformer, army manager game, the player must stratagise to fight the enemy team, using a mix of troops to deplete your enemies resources and conquer the empire.

To win this game, you'll need to use your strategic skills to push your army past the opponent's defences. Every troop that successfully reaches their base will gradually weaken their overall health. However, if the enemy manages to deplete your army's health, your troops will become increasingly enraged, resulting in a boost in both health and damage output.
