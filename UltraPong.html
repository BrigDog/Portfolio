2D top down arcade game, the player can try their luck with four different game modes, easy, medium, hard and offline PVP.

Most of my time making this game went towards allowing for a more customizable experience, this is my second game jam submission for the Mini Jam 130, Lunar Jam and the first game I've made which allows for custom bindings, custom art assets and custom settings.
